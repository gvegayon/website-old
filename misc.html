Realtime Analysis

What is talk about chilean presidential candidates in twitter (by Modularity.cl) with Jorge Fábrega, Pablo Paredes and Andrés Ortega. A small website that avery half hour retrives information from twitter about the Next Chilean Presidential Elections and analyses the contents dinamically
URL: http://www.modularity.cl/presidenciales

Data Visualization

Statistical Compendium CASEN (alpha august 30, 2011) Statistical Compendium of National Characterization Survey (CASEN) prepared jointly by the units “CASEN” and “Monitoreo de Programas Sociales” from the Ministry of Planning, current Ministry of Social Development. Using Google Public Data Explorer, we developed this cool visualization (in spanish and english) of several dimensions of about 50 georeferenced socio-demographic indicators measure since 1990 to 2009.
URL :http://www.google.com/publicdata/explore?ds=lt98u9rd734rn_&hl=en&dl=en

The evolution of chilean academic networks With Jorge Fábrega. Using the ISI web of knowledge, we retrived and process information on the last 60 years of research in Chile and produced this fancy dynamic graph that show the evolution of the chilean academic network since 1950′.
URL: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6H0veEmTgP0

#StopSOPA (Stop Internet Piracy Act). With Jorge Fábrega and Pablo Paredes. This graph here (follow the link) shows the mentions network of the first 30.000 tweets which included the hashtag #StopSOPA (from a total of 540 thousand) sent between january 17 and 18 of 2012. At the center you can see two big yellow circles, those are the accounts Fight For The Future (@fightfortheftr) and Anonymous (@YourAnonNews).
URL: http://nodos.modularity.cl/visual/stopsopa/

Deterministic Chaos While attending (as a hearer) the course “Physical and Mathematical Complexity” at Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez’s Ph.D program “Complex Systems Engineering”, I wrote this nice R-script that shows the evolution of a population growth model in 2D and 3D (using the rgl package). For this last (3D) it builds an animation and stores it as a GIF file.
URL: http://files.meetup.com/6789812/clase1_caos_determinista.zip
